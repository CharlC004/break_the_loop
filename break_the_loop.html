The Gnostic Principles The framework ▼
Reject
The world of consumption, status signaling, and empty spectacle. The Kenoma. The cage with gold bars.
Affirm
The divine spark within. Pneumatic fire. The capacity for gnosis — direct knowing, beyond belief or argument.
Recognize
The Archons: algorithms, rankings, dopamine loops, social mirrors. They rule through ignorance, not force.
Practice
Metanoia — the turning of the mind. Not once, but daily. Each moment of awareness is the spark catching light.

IIEvery hour reclaimed from the loop is an act of pneumatic rebellion. It does not need an audience.
IIIThe body is not the enemy of the spirit. The body trained, rested, and fed is the vessel the spark needs to burn.
IVArt that reaches the truth — Miura, Inoue, Dylan — is gnosis in another form. It lands before the mind can defend itself.
VDo nothing that is of no use. This is the razor. Apply it without mercy.
VIThe shadow is not destroyed by naming it. It is integrated. Guts does not defeat the berserker armor — he learns to contain it.
VIILoneliness that comes from depth is not the same as isolation. The solitary and elect shall find the kingdom.
